vorpal — PDF / EPUB / TXT → Audiobook
======================================

.. epigraph::

   He took his vorpal sword in hand:
   Long time the manxome foe he sought —
   And hast thou slain the Jabberwock?
   Come to my arms, my beamish boy!

   -- Lewis Carroll, *Jabberwocky* (1871)

**vorpal** converts book-shaped files into navigable ``.m4b`` audiobooks using
local, open-weight TTS models. The full pipeline — OCR, chapter detection,
normalisation, synthesis, loudness mastering — runs on your machine. No cloud
services, no accounts, no per-book cost beyond electricity and GPU time.

The name comes from the poem above. vorpal is the blade for the jabberwocky of
OCR noise, fake chapters, and screen-reader monotone.
